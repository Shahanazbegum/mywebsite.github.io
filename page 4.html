Activities

Ready for some outdoor fun? Explore our unique and wide-ranging experiences that are sure to thrill both the young and old.

Wildlife Tours

Make your visit extra wild! Go behind-the-scenes and get closer to our wildlife!

Learn how our animals are cared for, discover their unique traits and get the opportunity to feed some of them.

Keepers' Chit-Chat

Learn more about our wildlife from the people who know best - the keepers! Hear interesting quirks of the animals and get a chance to feed some of them yourselves.

Feed the Animals

Catch our animals when they're most active - during feeding sessions! Listen to our keepers share stories and even get to feed the animals yourself.

For Kids

A multitude of fun activities awaits kids! From a water playground for a splashing good time, close encounters with farmyard animals, to a wild carousel ride and many more!

Camps

Our camps encourage kids to explore with self-learning opportunities, team-bonding activities as well as unique insights into wildlife.
